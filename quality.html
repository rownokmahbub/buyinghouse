QUALITY CONTROL

NEXT EXPORT LTD puts a lot of emphasis on quality products and quality management. Therefore, the planned and systematic activities are implemented in a quality system to fulfill the garments' quality requirements. Our Quality Assurance also includes management of the quality of raw materials, fabric and color composition, products and components, services related to production, and management, production, and inspection processes. Our experience quality assurance teams manage all phases of product production using well-planned and designed quality systems, they not only conduct inspections but also follow a three-tier inspection system from when the orders are placed to when the product is ex-factory. This three-tier system includes Initial, Mid-line, and Final Inspections; as per the agreed upon AQL level. The quality Assurance process is carried out at each stage starting from the sample development. For testing, we work with BVCPS and SGS. This thorough set of standards, risk analysis, and building quality rigor ensures that buyer expectations are always met and often surpassed. We also undertake third-party quality inspections if required by the client as per the agreed or required AQL levels.
